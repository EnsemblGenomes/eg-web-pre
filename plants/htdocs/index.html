About Ensembl Plants Pre

Ensembl Plants Pre offers preliminary access to specific data pending full release in Ensembl Plants. Some data and tools available in Ensembl Plants may not be available for all species in pre.

Barley genome available in Ensembl Plants 'pre' site

A ten-nation consortium has reported the first high-quality reference genome sequence of barley. The barley genome was sequenced and assembled using an array of state-of- the-art methods, taking ten years. For the first time, scientists can now locate all genes precisely in the genome and analyze complex gene families that play a key role in malting and resilience.

New Bread Wheat Genome Assembly

The TGACv1 assembly of the wheat genome has now been incorporated in the main Ensembl Plants site. Please consult it there.
